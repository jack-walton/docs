# Buy Tickets

Complete the following steps to buy tickets.

<Steps>

## Select Offer

After you [search for an event](/AXS/search-for-events), tap **Select Offer**. Select either **Tickets** or, if available, **AXS Official Resale** to buy tickets from another user. Select the number of tickets that you want. Then, tap **Continue**. Select a method of delivery, then tap **Continue**.

![Step 1](/images/AXS/buy_tickets/buy_tickets_step_1.jpg)

## Enter Your Payment Information
    
Enter your credit card information, or select another available payment method. Optionally, select the checkbox to save this payment method for future purchases. 

![Step 6](/images/AXS/buy_tickets/buy_tickets_step_6.jpg)

When you have entered all your payment information, tap **Continue**.

## Complete Purchase

Enter your billing address, then tap **Continue**. Indicate whether you want to protect your purchase. Review your order information, the **Terms of Use**, and so on.

![Step 10](/images/AXS/buy_tickets/buy_tickets_step_10.jpg)

If you agree to the terms, select the checkbox and tap **Confirm Purchase**.

</Steps>
