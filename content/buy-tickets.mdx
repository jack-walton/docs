# Buy Tickets

Complete the following steps to buy tickets:

<Steps>
    ## After you search for an event, press **Select Offer**.

    ![Step 1](/images/AXS/buy_tickets/buy_tickets_step_1.jpg)

    ## Select either **Tickets** or, if available, **AXS Official Resale** to buy tickets from another user.
    ![Step 2](/images/AXS/buy_tickets/buy_tickets_step_2.jpg)

    ## Select the number of tickets that you want. Then, press **Continue**.
    ![Step 3](/images/AXS/buy_tickets/buy_tickets_step_3.jpg)

    ## Select a method of delivery, then press **Continue**.
    ![Step 4](/images/AXS/buy_tickets/buy_tickets_step_4.jpg)

    ## Enter your credit card information, or select another available payment method.
    
    ## Optionally, select the checkbox to save this payment method for future purchases.

    ![Step 6](/images/AXS/buy_tickets/buy_tickets_step_6.jpg)

    ## Tap **Continue**.

    ## Enter your billing address, then tap **Continue**.

    ## Indicate whether you want to protect your purchase.

    ## Review your order information, the **Terms of Use**, and so on.

    ![Step 10](/images/AXS/buy_tickets/buy_tickets_step_10.jpg)

    ## If you agree to the terms, select the checkbox and tap **Confirm Purchase**.

    ![Step 11](/images/AXS/buy_tickets/buy_tickets_step_11.jpg)

</Steps>
